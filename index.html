Fueling Fitness: The Ripped
Up Nutrition Success Story

Ripped Up Nutrition is a company that provides fitness enthusiasts with a variety of nutritional supplements to help them achieve their fitness goals. They offer a wide range of products including protein powders, BCAAs, pre-workout supplements, and more. Ripped Up Nutrition is committed to providing high-quality products that are made with natural ingredients. They also offer a variety of resources to help customers learn more about fitness and nutrition.

CHALLENGE

Ripped Up Nutrition faced issues like no online store, inefficient order management, limited payment options,
and lack of product authenticity verification.
They also had low customer engagement.

SOLUTION

The company solved these by launching an
e-commerce site, adding real-time order
tracking, secure payment options, and QR code verification for products.
They enhanced engagement with personalized
recommendations and fitness resources.

FUNCTIONALITY

  • User-Friendly website
  • Provides a variety of resources
  • Provides money back guarentee
    on all of their products
  • Customer can browse and purchase
    products

TECHNOLOGY STACK

  • React.js
  • Node.js
  • MongoDB
  • Stripe/Razorpay
  • Shiprocket APIs
  • AWS

Automating Wellness
with the
SmartMirror

Opportunity for the contact center operator to work with daily tasks as
efficiently as possible using a pre-configured workplace. In a single
window, an employee can easily process call queues, make mass
communications, share information in a corporate social network and
track their own performance indicators without switching between
sections.

CHALLENGE

    The Smart Mirror Company faced challenges
    in offering a unique product
    with advanced features, seamless user authentication, real
    -time weather updates, health monitoring
    tools, and entertainment integration.

SOLUTION

    To address these, they implemented a facial
    recognition login for personalized
    experiences, integrated real-time weather
    updates with suggestions, added health
    monitoring tools, and included notification
    management and music streaming. IoT
    integration connected the mirror with other
    smart devices.

FUNCTIONALITY

    and recommendations

    for optimal performance
